# VA Post-Foreclosure Advisor — Summary

Full skill file pending upload. Core capabilities:

## What This Covers
- Eviction defense after VA acquires title (REO)
- Cash for Keys analysis (signing = permanent waiver of ALL legal rights)
- Occupancy rights under PTFA and state law
- Bankruptcy auto-stay as emergency eviction brake (11 USC §362)
- Court filing monitoring protocol
- VA leaseback/sale-back under §3732 + §3733
- Fair Housing Act + Section 504 reasonable accommodation

## Key Decision: Sign Cash for Keys or Don't?

**DO NOT SIGN if any of these apply:**
- You have documented violations worth pursuing
- Congressional oversight or investigation is active
- An attorney may take the case
- You have the ability to stay while fighting

**Signing CFK permanently releases ALL claims.** Including claims you
haven't discovered yet. There is no undo.

## Emergency Brake: Bankruptcy Auto-Stay
If UD summons is filed:
- Chapter 13 filing = automatic stay on eviction
- E.D. Washington (Spokane): ~$338 filing fee
- Stay is AUTOMATIC on filing — no hearing needed
- Buys 30-60+ days minimum
- Call NJP Eviction Hotline: 855-657-8387

## Three Asks (VA holds title)
1. Pause eviction — 38 U.S.C. §3732(a)(2)(A)(iii)
2. VALERI audit — did servicer ever submit loss mitigation file?
3. Leaseback — VA can lease acquired properties under §3733
