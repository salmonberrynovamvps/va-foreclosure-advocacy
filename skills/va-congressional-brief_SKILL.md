## Partisan Framing Engine
Same facts, different emphasis:
- **Republican:** Failed promise, government waste, fiscal cost ($72K per foreclosure vs $0 partial claim)
- **Democratic:** Disparate impact, corporate pattern, housing equity
- **Bipartisan:** Oversight failure, fixable problem, accountability
